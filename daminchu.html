Daminchu is a curricular work when I first contacted the Service design and Business model.The teacher,Ying Hu,required us to design a realized service system for Intelligent Communities.It's a teamwork with 4 teamates and the part of me is to design the service system and calculate a sustainable business model.

TEAM WORK | Service System | Business model

Research

After research，we obtained and refined one market and two kinds of customers demand:

  • 1 \ People are carrying out food safety issues,more and more people want to eat safe home dishes.
  • 2 \ Someone wants to share his own meal with others to earn sense of identity and money.
  • 3 \ The traditional kitchen distribution method has high labor cost and low delivery efficiency.
